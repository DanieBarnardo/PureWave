# PureWave Business Profile

## Summary

PureWave is a premium independent home theatre consultancy serving Durban, South Africa and surrounding areas. The business is designed as a part-time or side consultancy with low overhead, selective client intake, and high-margin expert services.

## Business objective

- Build local authority in Durban for technically sophisticated home cinema design and calibration
- Generate supplemental income from specialized expertise
- Grow from evenings and weekends into a stronger premium consultancy over time

## Target clients

- Homeowners in affluent suburbs such as Westville, Umhlanga, and Hillcrest
- Clients with underperforming systems that need calibration, DSP tuning, or acoustic correction
- Homeowners planning a dedicated cinema room or major renovation
- Smart home enthusiasts who want integrated automation instead of fragmented remotes and controls

## Core services

### Acoustic design and calibration

- Room analysis based on dimensions and listening positions
- Acoustic treatment recommendations to reduce peaks, nulls, and response issues
- Advanced audio calibration beyond stock AVR auto-setup
- DSP configuration using measurement microphones and external processing where needed
- Subwoofer placement, low-frequency tuning, and enclosure guidance for cinema-grade bass

### Smart theatre integration

- Home Assistant-centered control design
- Lighting and playback-triggered automation
- Climate and air-quality integrations where useful for the theatre space
- Simplified control experiences that replace remote clutter

### System design and procurement advice

- Independent hardware consulting with no retail bias
- Projector and large-format screen planning, including 130-inch-plus setups
- Advice for local media playback ecosystems for high-bitrate or uncompressed content
- Design guidance that balances performance, usability, and future upgrade flexibility

## Competitive advantage

- Systems-thinking approach inspired by software architecture and engineering discipline
- Deep DIY and technical knowledge including fabrication, amplifier behavior, and active cooling modifications
- Independence from retail inventory and sales quotas
- Focus on bespoke design, calibration quality, and integration depth rather than basic installation work

## Service model

### The Blueprint

- A consultation package
- Usually a 2-hour on-site visit
- Includes evaluation of space, equipment, and client goals
- Followed by a detailed recommendations report
- Best framed as a fixed flat fee

### The Tuning

- Advanced calibration for existing systems
- Includes audio tuning, DSP configuration, and projector alignment as relevant
- Best framed as an hourly rate with a minimum session length

### The Director

- Full design and oversight engagement
- Covers end-to-end design, acoustic planning, automation logic, and contractor coordination
- Best framed as a retainer or percentage of total build cost

## Positioning language

PureWave should be presented as:

- Premium and independent
- Technical, bespoke, and client-first
- Focused on engineering-grade outcomes rather than sales markup
- Selective, detail-oriented, and high-trust

Avoid positioning PureWave as:

- A mass-market installer
- A discount equipment reseller
- A generic electrician or handyman service

## Go-to-market notes

- Build a portfolio using documented premium builds and calibration results
- Maintain a streamlined professional landing page with an engineering and automation angle
- Network with interior designers, architects, and high-end real estate contacts in Durban
