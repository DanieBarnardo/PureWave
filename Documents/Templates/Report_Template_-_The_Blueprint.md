## 9. Upgrade Sequencing

The recommended build sequence to protect the budget and avoid rework:

1. **Pre-wire everything** — speaker cables, HDMI runs, subwoofer lines, and conduit for future additions before walls or ceilings close
2. **Acoustic treatment** — before finalising speaker placement or running room EQ
3. **Front stage first** — AVR, L/C/R speakers, primary subwoofer
4. **Display / projection** — once room light conditions and screen wall are finalised
5. **Expand surrounds and heights** — once the foundation is performing well
6. **Automation integration** — once the core system is stable
