## 4. Project Phases

### Phase 1: Design & Pre-Wire
*Target completion: [Date]*

- Finalise room layout, screen wall position, and seating geometry
- Issue contractor-ready wiring schematic and conduit map
- Specify all cable types, runs, and termination points
- Confirm equipment selection and place orders for long-lead items
- Liaise with electrician and builder on pre-wire execution

**Key deliverable:** Wiring schematic, conduit map, and equipment order list

### Phase 2: Build & Acoustic Fit-Out
*Target completion: [Date]*

- Oversee acoustic treatment installation (panels, bass traps, diffusers)
- Confirm speaker rough-in positions against design intent
- Review ceiling, flooring, and wall finishes for acoustic impact
- Advise on rack design, ventilation, and cable management
- Sign off on pre-wire before walls and ceilings are sealed

**Key deliverable:** Build sign-off checklist and acoustic treatment specification

### Phase 3: Commissioning & Calibration
*Target completion: [Date]*

- Equipment installation and system integration
- Advanced audio calibration — speaker levels, distances, crossovers, DSP, room EQ
- Projector alignment, screen geometry, and display calibration
- Automation programming — lighting scenes, playback macros, environmental triggers
- Client handover — walkthrough, training, and documentation

**Key deliverable:** Commissioned system, automation scripts, and client handover report
